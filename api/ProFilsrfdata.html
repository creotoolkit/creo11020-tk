Object ProFilsrfdata

Fillet surface data

Superobjects:

ProSurfacedata

A fillet surface is found where a round or a fillet is placed on a curved edge, or on an edge with non-constant arc radii. On a straight edge, you would use a cylinder to represent the fillet.
